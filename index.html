Our Story

A journey of passion and tradition

Welcome to Yukai Matcha, where passion for authentic matcha meets cozy ambiance. Our journey began with a simple love for the calming ritual of preparing and enjoying matcha.

We carefully source our matcha directly from small farms in Japan, ensuring the highest quality and most authentic experience for our customers.

Every product in our shop is selected with care, focusing on sustainability and traditional preparation methods that honor the rich history of matcha.
